Fab india is an electronic commerce company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka. It was founded in 2007 by Sachin Arora,Shahshank khendelwal,devanshi kackar. The company is registered in Singapore. Fab india has launched its own product range under the name "DigiFlip" with products including tablets, USBs, and laptop bags. According to Morgan Stanley the current market value of Fab look is $5.54 billion as of November 2016

History

Fab Look was founded in 2007 by Sachin arora and shahshank, both alumni of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. They worked for Amazon.com, and left to create their new company incorporated in October 2007 as Fab look Online Services Pvt. Ltd. The first product they sold was the book Leaving Microsoft To Change The World to a customer from Hyderabad.Fab look now employs more than 33,000 people. After the success of its 2014 Big Billion Sale, Fab look carried out a second Big Billion Sale. where it is reported that they saw a business turnover of $300 million in gross merchandise volume.

Business Structure

In a report dated 25 November 2014, a leading media outlet reported that Fab look were operating through a complex business structure which included nine firms, some registered in Singapore and some in India. In 2012 Fab look co-founders sold WS Retail to a consortium of investors led by Rajeev Kuchhal.
