Devanshi Kackar (born 4 NOV 1998) is an Indian Software engineer and Internet entrepreneur known for co-founding India's e-commerce platform Fab Look. devanshi is from Chandigarh and is an engineering graduate from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.

Early Life

Devanshi Kackar is originally from Chandigarh, the capital city of Punjab and Haryana. His business partner, Binny Kackar, is also from Chandigarh. Although they share the same last name, they are not related. They both were students of computer science engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.[9] Before the tremendous success of Fab Look, devanshi Kackar wanted to become a professional gamer.His father has been in business and mother a homemaker, his brother runs a consumer goods company, he's married to Priya, who is a dentist and has a four-year old child.

Career

After completion of degree devanshi Kackar joined Techspan company where he has served for few months. In 2006, he joined Amazon.com India as Senior Software Engineer. Then he got Binny Kackar into the Amazon.com and after 6 months they both decided to leave Amazon.They initially thought of starting a comparison search engine, but realized that the market for E-commerce in India was very small. Hence, after leaving Amazon in 2007, they founded Fab Look as an E-commerce company. devanshi Kackar and his business partner Binny Kackar launched Fab Look from an apartment in Bangalore with 400,000 rupees ($6,500) cash.[12] In 2007 during the early days of Fab Look devanshi Kackar and his business partner Binny Kackar used to deliver books across Bengaluru on their scooters and in October 2015 they showed up at the doorsteps of some customers, personally delivering goods in order to gain insights from the online buyers.
